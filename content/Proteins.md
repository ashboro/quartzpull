# Proteins

- Composed of amino acids
  - Amino group, carboxyl group, R group
- Levels of structure:
  - Primary: sequence of amino acids
  - Secondary: α-helix, β-pleated sheet (hydrogen bonds)
  - Tertiary: 3D folding (disulfide, ionic, hydrophobic interactions)
  - Quaternary: multiple polypeptides (e.g., hemoglobin)
- Peptide bonds via condensation
- Biuret test (purple color for proteins)
