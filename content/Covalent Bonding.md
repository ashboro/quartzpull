# Covalent Bonding

- Strong stable bonds with H, O, N, P, S
- Example: Methane (CH₄) — tetrahedral molecule
- Enables formation of macromolecules
- Bonds formed via condensation reactions
  - Releases water
  - Forms glycosidic, peptide, ester bonds
