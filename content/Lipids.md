# Lipids

- Triglycerides = glycerol + 3 fatty acids
  - Formed via ester bonds
  - Insoluble, energy-dense
- Phospholipids
  - Hydrophilic head, hydrophobic tails
  - Form bilayers in membranes
- Roles:
  - Energy storage
  - Insulation
  - Protection (e.g., kidneys)
  - Structural (membranes)
